Chaff

Hide a private message inside ordinary-looking text. The model runs entirely in your browser — no server, no account, and it works offline once loaded.

Cover text reads as normal to people and keyword filters.

How private is this?
Chaff hides that a message is there from casual or automated review — not from a determined adversary. Someone who has this exact model and specifically suspects steganography can detect it statistically. Your safety rests on the shared password staying secret and on not being individually targeted by a matched-model detector. Treat it as cover, not as a guarantee.
